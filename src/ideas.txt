move with arrows or numpad
attach with arrows or numpad

modifier keys:
w to run
q to walk slowly
d to push
s to pull

interact keys:
space to pickup
i for inventory
l to look (starts at your char)
numpad 5 to rest
. to rest


doors are +, open doors are /. 
Some enemies can not open doors.
When next to an open door, pull or push to close.
